Strategy + Production
SVNCRWNS is a strategy and production company that started with the single mission of bridging the gap between community and commerce through creative and sustainable engineering. Since its inception, SVNCRWNS has partnered with local businesses in and around Baltimore, MD working to provide strategy, youth programming and the execution of special projects including art installations, film, photography and digital marketing.
